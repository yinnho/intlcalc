How to convert gigahertz to hertz

1GHz = 109Hz

or

1Hz = 10-9GHz

Gigahertz to hertz formula

The frequency f in hertz (Hz) is equal to the frequency f in gigahertz (GHz) times 109:

f(Hz) = f(GHz) × 109

Example

Convert 3 gigahertz to hertz:

f(Hz) = 3GHz × 109 = 3×109Hz
