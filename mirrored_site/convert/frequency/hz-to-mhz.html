How to convert hertz to megahertz

1Hz = 0.000001MHz

or

1MHz = 1000000Hz

Hertz to megahertz formula

The frequency f in megahertz (MHz) is equal to the frequency f in hertz (Hz) divided by 1000000:

f(MHz) = f(Hz) / 1000000

Example

Convert 300 hertz to megahertz:

f(MHz) = 300Hz / 1000000 = 0.0003MHz
