How to convert megahertz to kilohertz

1kHz = 0.001MHz

or

1MHz = 1000kHz

Megahertz to kilohertz formula

The frequency f in kilohertz (kHz) is equal to the frequency f in megahertz (MHz) times 1000:

f(kHz) = f(MHz) × 1000

Example

Convert 3 megahertz to kilohertz:

f(kHz) = 3MHz × 1000 = 3000kHz
