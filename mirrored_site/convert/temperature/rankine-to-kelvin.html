How to convert Rankine to Kelvin

The temperature T in Kelvin (K) is equal to the temperature T in Rankine (°R) times 5/9:

T(K) = T(°R) × 5/9

Example

Convert 300 degrees Rankine to Kelvin:

T(K) = 300°R × 5/9 = 166.67 K
