How to convert Rankine to Celsius

The temperature T in degrees Celsius (°C) is equal to the temperature T in Rankine (°R) times 5/9, minus 273.15:

T(°C) = T(°R) × 5/9 - 273.15

Example

Convert 300 degrees Rankine to degrees Celsius:

T(°C) = 300°R × 5/9 - 273.15= -106.48 °C
