How to convet volts to kilovolts

1 V = 10-3 kV = 0.001 kV

or

1 kV = 103 V = 1000 V

Volts to kilovolts formula

The voltage V in kilovolts (kV) is equal to the voltage V in volts (V) divided by 1000:

V(kV) = V(V) / 1000

Example

Convert 3 volts to kilovolts:

V(kV) = 3V / 1000 = 0.003 kV
