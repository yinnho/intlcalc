How to convert meters to yards

1 meter is equal to 1.093613298 yards:

1m = 100cm/(2.54cm/in)/(36in/yd) = 1.093613298yd

The distance d in yards (yd) is equal to the distance d in meters (m) divided by 0.9144:

d(yd) = d(m) / 0.9144

Example

Convert 20 m to yards:

d(yd) = 20m / 0.9144 = 21.872266yd

How many yards in a meter

One meter is equal to 1.093613298 yards:

1m = 1m / 0.9144yd/m = 1.093613298yd

How many meters in a yard

One yard is equal to 0.9144 meters:

1yd = 0.9144×1yd = 0.9144m

How to convert 10m to yards

Multiply 10 meters by 0.9144 to get yards:

10m = 10m / 0.9144m/yd = 10.9361yd
