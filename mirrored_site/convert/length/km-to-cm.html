How to convert kilometers to centimeters

1 kilometer is equal to 100000 centimeters:

1km = 100000cm

The distance d in centimeters (cm) is equal to the distance d in kilometers (km) times 100000:

d(cm) = d(km) × 100000

Example

Convert 20 kilometers to centimeters:

d(cm) = 20km × 100000 = 2000000cm

How many centimeters in a kilometer

One kilometer is equal to 100000 centimeter:

1km = 1km×100000 = 100000cm

How many kilometers in a centimeter

One centimeter is equal to 0.00001 kilometers:

1cm = 1cm/100000 = 0.00001km

How to convert 8 kilometers to centimeters

Multiply 8 kilometers by 100000 to get centimeters:

8km = 100000×8km = 800000cm
