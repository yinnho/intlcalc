How to convert yards to centimeters

1 yard is equal to 91.44 centimeters:

1yd = 91.44cm

The distance d in centimeters (cm) is equal to the distance d in yards (yd) times 91.44:

d(cm) = d(yd) × 91.44

Example

Convert 20 yards to centimeters:

d(cm) = 20yd × 91.44 = 1828.8cm

How many yards in a centimeter

One centimeter is equal to 109.36 yards:

1cm = 1cm / (91.44cm/yd) = 109.36yd

How many centimeters in a yards

One yard is equal to 91.44 centimeters:

1yd = 91.44×1yd = 91.44cm

How to convert 10 yards to centimeters

Multiply 10 yards by 91.44 to get centimeters:

10yd = 91.44×10yd = 914.4cm
