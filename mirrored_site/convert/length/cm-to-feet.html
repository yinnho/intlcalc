Centimeter

Centimeter (cm) or centimetre is a unit of length in the International System of Units (SI). Centi is a prefix for 1/100.
One centimeter is equal to 1/100 of a meter (m).
One centimeter is equal to 10 millimeters (mm).
One centimeter is equal to 1/2.54 of an inch (in).

Foot

Foot (ft) is a unit of length.
One foot is equal to 12 inches (in).
One foot is equal to 1/3 of a yard (yd).
One foot is equal to 30.48 centimeters (cm).

How to convert centimeters to feet

1 centimeter is equal to 0.0328084 feet:

1cm = (1/2.54/12)ft = 0.0328084ft

The distance d in feet (ft) is equal to the distance d in centimeters (cm) divided by 30.48:

d(ft) = d(cm) / 30.48

Or

feet = centimeters / 30.48

Example #1

Convert 20 cm to feet:

20cm = 20cm / 30.48cm/ft = 0.656168ft

Example #2

Convert 50 cm to feet:

50cm = 50cm / 30.48cm/ft = 1.64042ft

How many feet in a centimeter

One centimeter is equal to 0.0328084 feet:

1cm = 1cm / 30.48cm/ft = 0.0328084ft

How many centimeters in a foot

One foot is equal to 30.48 centimeters:

1ft = 30.48×1ft = 30.48cm

How to convert 10cm to feet

Divide 10 centimeters by 30.48 to get feet:

10cm = 10cm / 30.48cm/ft = 0.328084ft
