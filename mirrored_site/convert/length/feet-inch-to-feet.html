How to convert feet+inches to feet

1 foot is equal to 12 inches:

1ft = 12″

The distance d in feet (ft) is equal to the distance d in feet (ft) plus the distance d in inches (″) divided by 12:

d(ft) = d(ft) + d(″)/12

Example

Convert 2 feet + 3 inches to feet:

d(ft) = 2ft + 3″/12 = 2.25ft
