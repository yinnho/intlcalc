How to convert inches to feet+inches

1 inch is equal to 1/12 feet:

1″ = 1/12ft = 0.083333ft

The distance d in feet (ft) is equal to the floor integer value of the distance d in inches (″) divided by 12:

d(ft) = ⌊d(″) / 12⌋

The additional distance d in inches (″) is equal to the whole distance d in inches (″) minus 12 times the integer disatance d in feet (ft):

dadd(″) = d(″) - 12×d(ft)

Example

Convert 20 inches to feet+inches:

d(ft) = ⌊20″/12⌋ = 1ft

dadd(″) = 20″-12×1ft = 8in
