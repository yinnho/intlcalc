Meter

Meter (m) or metre is a unit of length in the International System of Units (SI).
One meter is equal to 100 of a centimeters (cm).
One meter is equal to 1000 millimeters (mm).
One meter is equal to 100/2.54 inch (in).

Foot

Foot (ft) is a unit of length.
One foot is equal to 12 inches (in).
One foot is equal to 1/3 of a yard (yd).
One foot is equal to 0.3048 meter (m).

How to convert meters to feet

1 meter is equal to 3.280839895 feet:

1m = 100cm/(2.54cm/in)/(12in/ft) = 3.280839895ft

The distance d in feet (ft) is equal to the distance d in meters (cm) times 3.280839895:

d(ft) = d(m) × 3.280839895

Example

Convert 20 m to feet:

d(ft) = 20m × 3.280839895 = 65.6167979ft

How many feet in a meter

One meter is equal to 3.280839895 feet:

1m = 1m × 3.280839895ft/m = 3.280839895ft

How many meters in a foot

One foot is equal to 0.3048 meters:

1ft = 0.3048m/ft × 1ft = 0.3048m

How to convert 10m to feet

Multiply 10 meters by 0.3048 to get feet:

10m = 10m / 0.3048m/ft = 32.80839895ft
