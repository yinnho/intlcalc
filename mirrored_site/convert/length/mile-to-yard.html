How to convert miles to yards

1 mile is equal to 1760 yards:

1mi = 1760yd

The distance d in yards (yd) is equal to the distance d in miles (mi) times 1760:

d(yd) = d(mi) × 1760

Example

Convert 20 mi to yards:

d(yd) = 20mi × 1760 = 35200yd
