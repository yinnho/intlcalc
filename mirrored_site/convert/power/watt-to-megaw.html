How to convert watts to megawatts

1W = 0.000001MW

1MW = 1000000W

Watts to megawatts conversion formula

The power in megawatts P(MW) is equal to the power in watts P(W) divided by 1000000:

P(MW) = P(W) / 1000000

Example

Convert 5W to megawatts:

P(MW) = 5W / 1000000 = 0.000005MW
