How to convert kilowatts to milliwatts

The power in milliwatts P(mW) is equal to one million times the power in kilowatts P(kW):

P(mW) = 1000000 × P(kW)

 

So

1kW = 1000000mW

Example

Convert 1.3 kilowatts to milliwatts:

P(mW) = 1000000 × 1.3kW = 1300000mW
