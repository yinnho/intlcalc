How to convert Stones to Pounds

1 stone (st) is equal to 14 pounds (lb).

1 st = 14 lb

The mass m in pounds (lb) is equal to the mass m in stones (st) times 14:

m(lb) = m(st) × 14

Example

Convert 5 st to pounds:

m(lb) = 5 st × 14 = 70 lb
