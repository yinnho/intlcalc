How to convert Pounds to Stones

1 pound (lb) is equal to 0.0714286 stones (st).

1 lb = 0.0714286 st

The mass m in stones (st) is equal to the mass m in pounds (lb) divided by 14:

m(st) = m(lb) / 14

Example

Convert 5 lb to stones:

m(st) = 5 lb / 14 = 0.357 st
