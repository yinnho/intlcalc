How to convert Pounds to Grams

1 pound (lb) is equal to 453.59237 grams (g).

1 lb = 453.59237 g

The mass m in grams (g) is equal to the mass m in pounds (lb) times 453.59237:

m(g) = m(lb) × 453.59237

Example

Convert 5 lb to grams:

m(g) = 5 lb × 453.59237 = 2268 g
