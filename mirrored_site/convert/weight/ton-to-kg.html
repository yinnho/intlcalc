How to convert Tons to Kilograms

1 ton (t) is equal to 1000 kilograms (kg).

1 t = 1000 kg

The mass m in kilograms (kg) is equal to the mass m in ton (t) times 1000:

m(kg) = m(t) × 1000

Example

Convert 5t to kilograms:

m(kg) = 5 t × 1000 = 5000 kg
