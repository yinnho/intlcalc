How to convert eV to joules

One electron-volt is equal to 1.602176565⋅10-19 joules:

1eV = 1.602176565e-19 J = 1.602176565⋅10-19 J

 

So the energy in joules E(J) is equal to the energy in electron-volts E(eV) times 1.602176565⋅10-19:

E(J) = E(eV) × 1.602176565⋅10-19
