How to convert joules to kWh

One joule is equal to 2.777778⋅10-7 kilowatt-hours:

1J = 2.777778⋅10-7kWh = (1/3600000)kWh

 

So the energy in kilowatt-hour E(kWh) is equal to the energy in joules E(J) divided by 3600000:

E(kWh) = E(J) / 3600000 = E(J) ⋅ 2.777778⋅10-7
