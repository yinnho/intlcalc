How to convert coulombs to ampere-hours

1C = 2.7778⋅10-4Ah

or

1Ah = 3600C

Coulombs to ampere-hours formula

The charge in ampere-hours Q(Ah) is equal to the charge in coulombs Q(C) divided by 3600:

Q(Ah) = Q(C) / 3600

Example

Convert 3 coulombs to ampere-hours:

Q(Ah) = 3C / 3600 = 8.333⋅10-4Ah
