How to convert coulombs to microcoulombs

1C = 1000000μC

or

1μC = 0.000001C

Coulombs to microcoulombs formula

The charge in microcoulombs Q(μC) is equal to the charge in coulombs Q(C) times 1000000:

Q(μC) = Q(C) × 1000000

Example

Convert 3 coulombs to microcoulombs:

Q(μC) = 3C × 1000000 = 3000000μC
