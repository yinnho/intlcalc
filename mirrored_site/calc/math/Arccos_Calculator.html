Arccos definition

The arccosine function is the inverse function of cos(x).

arccos(x) = cos-1(x)

 

For example, If the cosine of 60° is 0.5:

cos(60°) = 0.5

Then the arccos of 0.5 is 60°:

arccos(0.5) = cos-1(0.5) = 60°
