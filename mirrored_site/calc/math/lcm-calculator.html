LCM example

Find the least common multiple number for numbers 8 and 12:

The multiples of 8 are:

8, 16, 24, 32, 40, ...

The multiples of 12 are:

12, 24, 36, 48, ...

So the least common multiple number is 24:

lcm(8,12) = 24
