Discount calculation

Discount amount = Discount (%) × Initial price

Final price = (100% - Discount (%)) × Initial price

Final price = Initial price - Discount amount

Example

Initial price=$100, Discount (%)=10%

Discount amount = 10% × $100 = $90

Final price = (100% - 10%) × $100 = $90

Final price = $100 - $10 = $90
